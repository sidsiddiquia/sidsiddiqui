About

Bachelor’s in the Science of Nursing holder from Purdue University with a strong academic background in nursing combined with over five years of supervisory and medical experience in nursing health care, ranging from both acute to sub-acute clinical environments.  I am BLS, ACLS, and NIHSS certified, as well as proficient with a variety of nursing specialties.  Regarded as focused and avid learner with a responsible work ethic, which would be well suited for many different professional opportunities.
